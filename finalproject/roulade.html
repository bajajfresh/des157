Polish Roulade

Ingredients

    The roulade has a beautiful, spring green color. Made with spinach, cheese and cream.

  • 5 tablespoons Hortex shredded spinach,(thawed)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 3 eggs
  • 5 tablespoons flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 300g cream cheese
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 yellow pepper
  • 1 green pepper
  • 3 radish
  • 2 tablespoons parsley
  • 2 stalks fresh dill

Place the cheese in a bowl. Peel the peppers and finely dice them. Cut the radish into cubes. Chop the dill. Add all ingredients to the bowl with cheese and mix thoroughly. Season with salt and pepper. Put the cream cheese evenly on the cooled cake. Roll up the roulade by removing the parchment. Put the ready dish in the fridge.
